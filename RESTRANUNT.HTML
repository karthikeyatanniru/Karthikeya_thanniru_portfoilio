Building a Restaurant

In this project, I leveraged Building a Restranunt Website

Here are the steps the way build a Restranunt Website using HTMl,css and Bootstrap Framework.-

*Header Section: Include the restaurant logo and name. Navigation bar with links to different sections like Home, Menu, About Us, Contact, etc.

*Hero Section: Large image or video background showcasing the restaurant ambiance or popular dishes. message or slogan overlaid on the image.

*About Us Section: Brief description of the restaurant, its history, and mission. Photos of the interior, chefs, or staff to add a personal touch.

*Menu Section: Categorized menu items with images, descriptions, and prices. Option to filter by category (e.g., appetizers, main courses, desserts). Add-to-cart functionality for online ordering

*Specials or Promotions Section: Highlight any ongoing specials, deals, or promotions. Include enticing images or descriptions to attract attention.

*Testimonials Section: Customer reviews or testimonials to build trust and credibility. Include photos or names of satisfied customers (with permission).

*Contact Section: Contact form for inquiries, reservations, or feedback. Address, phone number, and email for direct contact. Social media icons linking to the restaurant's profiles.

*Footer Section: Copyright information. Links to privacy policy, terms of service, etc. Additional navigation links (e.g., Careers, Press, Blog).

*Responsive Design: Ensure the website is mobile-friendly using Bootstrap's responsive grid system. Test on various devices to ensure optimal display and functionality.
